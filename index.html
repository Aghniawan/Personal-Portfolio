About Me

Hello, I am a fresh graduate with a Bachelor’s degree in Accounting from Gunadarma University, with a strong passion for finance and business, particularly in accounting and financial management. Throughout my studies, I gained comprehensive knowledge in various aspects of accounting, including financial accounting, management accounting, cost accounting, taxation, and auditing.
